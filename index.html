Getting Started What ages do you serve? We serve children from infancy through elementary age, including our early learning, afterschool, and summer enrichment programs. What are your hours of operation? We offer extended care to support working families, with programming that runs from morning through early evening. How do I enroll my child? Families can complete our online registration form. Once submitted, our team will follow up with next steps and enrollment confirmation. Is there a registration fee? Yes, there is a one time registration fee to secure your child’s placement. Programs & Learning What does a typical day look like? Each day includes a balance of academic support, enrichment, outdoor play, meals or snacks, and social emotional learning experiences. What enrichment programs do you offer? Scholars engage in math, reading, STEAM and STEM, music, Spanish, physical education, swimming, gardening, and hands on experiences including trips and in program events. Do you provide homework help? Yes, we support scholars with homework and provide additional academic reinforcement when needed. Social Emotional Learning & Behavior How do you support student behavior? We use restorative practices and social emotional learning to guide behavior. This means we teach children how to understand their emotions, communicate effectively, and repair harm when challenges arise. What happens if there is a conflict between students? We address conflict through restorative circles and guided conversations, helping children take responsibility, reflect, and rebuild relationships in a supportive way. Health & Safety How do you keep children safe? Safety is our top priority. We maintain structured supervision, secure check in and dismissal procedures, and trained staff to ensure every child is safe and cared for. Do you handle medical needs or allergies? Yes, families can share medical information during enrollment, and we take all necessary precautions to support children’s health needs. Are staff trained and certified? Yes, our team includes experienced and trained educators who are committed to high quality care and learning. Tuition & Policies How does tuition work? Tuition is billed weekly or monthly depending on your program. Payments are due in advance to secure your child’s spot. Do you charge for absences? Yes, tuition remains consistent even if a child is absent, as it secures their placement in the program. Do you charge during holidays or closures? We do not charge for major scheduled school closures such as winter break or spring break. Family Partnership How do you communicate with families? We use direct messaging, email, and program updates to keep families informed and connected. Can families be involved? Absolutely. We welcome family engagement and believe strong partnerships support student success.
